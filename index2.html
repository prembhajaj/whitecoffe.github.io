About Us


White Coffee is a premiere event management firm that specializes in creating fully immersive impactful experiences for our clients. We offer our clients support on all fronts of hosting events – from conceptualization to execution. Our team understands the need for individual expression in big scale events and works closely with our clients to personalize their events. We do not believe in putting cookie-cutter events and strive to tailor events to our clients’ needs and ideas.


Our team identifies key influencers in various sectors and arranges tie-ups beneficial to our clients. We believe in forging meaningful connections. Our events are planned to create an unforgettable and meaningful experience for our clients’ audience. We are equipped to handle events of all scales and provide relevant publicity for them. We are storytellers who plan and execute beautiful and comprehensive events for our clients.
